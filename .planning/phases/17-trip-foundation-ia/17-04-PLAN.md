<objective>
Flip the Information Architecture from the global 5-tab `(tabs)/{boards,discover,me,friends,new}` to the trip-scoped 4-tab `trip/[id]/(tabs)/{map,plan,book,ledger}`, wire the 0/1/N entry branch (NAV-01), build the trip-scoped Stack header with trip-switch + profile and remove the FAB (NAV-03), port the existing board map content under the map tab, add plan/book/ledger tab screens, repoint the share ingestion flow to `/trip/{id}/plan`, and delete the old `boards/` + global `(tabs)` routes — all in ONE plan (Pitfall 2: index/share-handler/native-intent must change together or the save flow splits between trip and board routes).

Purpose: This is the user-visible IA reframe. The biggest risk is silent regression (Pitfall 1): mixing header+tabs in one layout or double-nesting `(tabs)` makes the tab bar vanish; fixing share-handler without index leaves the save flow broken.
Output: the full trip-scoped navigation shell with all 4 tab screens, the entry branch, the header, a repointed share flow; `boards/` + global `(tabs)` removed; `me` preserved as a trip-out screen.
</objective>
