<objective>
Wire live chat + presence into the existing single-channel island (CHAT-01/02). This is the highest-risk integration in the phase â€” the binding-order rule is load-bearing.

Purpose: The `trip_messages` INSERT binding MUST be chained onto the island's existing `moa:{tripId}` channel BEFORE `.subscribe()` (postgres_changes bindings negotiate at subscribe time; a binding added after subscribe delivers nothing and can throw `CHANNEL_ERROR` mismatch). Tab switching is client-state so the island mounts once and the channel is never torn down (D-02). History persists across refresh via a server-side `listTripMessages` seed.
